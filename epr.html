What is EPR?


Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) is a concept where manufacturers, importers, and sellers of products take responsibility for the entire life cycle of their products, especially regarding proper disposal and recycling. This means that those who produce goods are accountable for the environmental impact of those goods, even after they are sold and used.
