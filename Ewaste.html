Sources of E Waste
Electronic waste (E-Waste) can originate from various sources, spanning both households and businesses.
The primary sources of E-Waste include:

  • Consumer Electronics

    • Mobile Phone

    • Computers and Laptops

    • Tablets and E-Readers

    • Cameras and Camcorders

  • Home Appliances

    • Refrigerators and Freezers

    • Washing Machines and Dryers

    • Microwaves

    • Lighting Equipment

  • Entertainment Devices

    • Televisions

    • Audio Equipment

    • Tablets and E-Readers

  • Office Equipment

    • Printers and Copiers

    • Fax Machines

  • IT and Communication Equipment

    • Networking Equipment

    • Landline Phones

  • Medical Devices

    • Diagnostic Equipment

  • Industrial and Commercial Electronics

    • Control Panels and Automation Equipment

    • Office Electronics
